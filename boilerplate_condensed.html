Opa! We've created this chicken dish to pay homage to traditional Spanakopita, but with its own twist. We start with grilled chicken breast marinated in shawarma spices, and surround it with a bed of spanakopita-inspired spinach basmati rice. On the side is a lemon wedge and a traditional veggie raita sauce with onions, carrots, cucumbers, and mint.
